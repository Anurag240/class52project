Interesting Facts about Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Most Cicadas have red-orange eyes.
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons have strong muscles used for flying.
  • Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour.
  • Pigeons managed to reach speed on 92 miles per hour.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • There are over 160 words for camel in Arabic alone.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour
  • Camels can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Their feet work like salad tongs.
  • Almost half of all known species live in madagascar.
  • Skin crystals enable them to change color at will.
  • Unlike many lizards, chameleons can't regrow their tails.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear

  • Bear hugs predate teddy bears by 60 years.
  • The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  • Winnie the Pooh was based on a real bear.
  • They’re the best-traveled toys.
